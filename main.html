How It Works

1. Report an Issue

Submit a real-world problem, like illegal dumping or a water leak, via our dashboard.

2. Agent Analyzes & Plans

Our AI agent perceives the data, analyzes its severity, and forms a multi-step plan.

3. Autonomous Action

The agent takes action: dispatching help, alerting NGOs, or contacting municipalities.
